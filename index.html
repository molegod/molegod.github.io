I am also the founder and lead of Cornell XR, the fastest club to ever become an official Cornell Engineering project team building projects in Haptics and XR.

Outside of my academics, I enjoy creative work, building weird tech, and making satirical designs. I've been commisioned to make 3D-printed sculptures and other works before.
